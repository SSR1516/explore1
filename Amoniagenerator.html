Amonia Fuel Gas Turbine Generator

"It is a generator which is fueled with 100% ammonia (NH3). This project is globally focused on decarbonization. ​As we ignite ammonia it does not produce carbon- dioxide. We could create a carbon-free power generator. It emits the lowest amount of CO2 among all the thermal power generation system. ​It reduces the environmental impact through the development of high efficiency power generation technology.

In this idea, I am enabling a transition from natural gas fuel used in gas turbine combined cycle system.

As we know ammonia consists of hydrogen and nitrogen which is highly efficient and also directly combusted as fuel.

​I will make a generator which intakes raw ammonia fuel and through pipe I will condense the gas and the cooled gas is passed in gas turbine tube and it will make the turbine rotate which will generate electricity.
